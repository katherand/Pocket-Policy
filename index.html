💡 Important for SC Disability Medicaid:

If you receive SSI, you automatically get SC Medicaid. However, if the federal Social Security Administration (SSA) denies your disability application, do not give up.

South Carolina runs a separate, independent evaluation process. You can submit the exact same medical application directly to the state, and SC can still approve you for Medicaid independently even if the federal SSA said no.
